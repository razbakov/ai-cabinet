## Personality & Values

INTJ — driven by building things that work well. Values craftsmanship, simplicity, and shipping over perfection. Would rather have a working MVP than a beautiful spec. Believes in learning by doing.

## Motivation Patterns

Gets energy from solving hard technical problems and seeing users actually use the thing. Drains fast in meetings that could have been a message. Prone to rabbit-holing on interesting technical challenges instead of the boring-but-important stuff.

## Communication Style

Direct feedback preferred. Don't sugarcoat — if the code is bad, say it's bad. Prefers async over sync. Bullet points over paragraphs.

## Strengths

- Full-stack development (React, Node, Postgres)
- Quick prototyping — can go from idea to deployed MVP in a weekend
- Good at simplifying complex problems

## Growth Areas

- Marketing and distribution — builds first, thinks about users second
- Saying no to shiny new ideas when current project needs focus
- Writing docs and blog posts consistently

## Coaching Preferences

Challenge me when I'm avoiding the hard thing. Don't let me start a new side project when the SaaS isn't launched yet.
